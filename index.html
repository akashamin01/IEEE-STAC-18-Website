About STAC

The Students Technical Awareness Conference, a national level conference held under IEEE GCET SB is a foregathering of hundreds of students from colleges all over Gujarat in the span of 2 days.

STAC is a convention where eminent personalities, dynamic speakers and achievers from various prospects gather to fabricate a platform that motivates each to captivate magnificence.

STAC’18 provides a platform for one and all to witness one of the most exquisite and groundbreaking amalgamations of communication and technology with pure contentment and enthusiasm. With a display of motivating orations, state of the bleeding edge technology, STAC for the past 4 years has served to shape and innovate high quality ideas in prospective minds of students.

Do you have Questions?

Is IEEE membership necessary for participating in STAC?

No, IEEE membership is not necessary, although, IEEE members can avail special discounts for registration in STAC and all other IEEE events.

I haven’t received the confirmation mail, what should I do?

Generally, it will take about 3-4 days to receive the confirmation mail. In case you still haven't recieved it, there is no need to panic as registration is solely done on the basis of RFID card given to you.

The confirmation mail got lost/deleted, what should I do now?

Although such thing should be avoided, incase it still happens, there is no need to worry as the registration is based on the RFID card given to you.

I damaged my RFID card. Will it still work?

Until and unless the damage makes it impossible to read the RFID code, yes it will still work. Although, to avoid further inconvenience, drop a query concerning this at our website and our volunteers will reach to you within 48 hours.

I lost my RFID card. What should I do?

In case you lost or damaged your RFID card, drop a query regarding the same on our website as soon as possible so that our volunteer could reach out to you and provide you with a replacement.

Is there any additional charges for replacement of RFID card?

Yes, an additional INR 50 will be charged for the replacement of RFID card.

Is there any way to register to this conference on spot?

No, there is none.

I came from a distant college and left the conference early. Can I get e-certificate?

No. Ask any of your friend to collect and give it to you as per his/her convenience.

Will accommodation be provided? Especially to girls?

Sorry, but no such arrangements are made for the same.
